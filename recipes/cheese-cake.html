Cheese Cake

Simple Information

  • Prep Time : 20 mins
  • Cook Time : 50 mins
  • Total Time : 1 hr 10 mins
  • Servings : 24
  • Yield : 1 9x13-inch cake

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 24 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 cup confectioner's sugar for dusting

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch pan.

Step 2

Make yellow cake mix according to package directions. Pour batter into the prepared pan.

Step 3

Mix together the ricotta cheese, sugar, eggs and vanilla extract and spoon over cake batter.

Step 4

Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 45 minutes. Sprinkle cake with confectioners' sugar when cool.
